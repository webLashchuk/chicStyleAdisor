Personal Experience

Welcome to the world of style! Hailing from the beautiful city of San Francisco, I've always been surrounded by a creative atmosphere and a variety of fashion trends. My interest in fashion and style began in early childhood, spending hours poring over magazines and fashion shows. This innate interest led me to keep up with trends and become a stylist, helping people express themselves through their style.

My passion for style was further fueled by participating in numerous events and collaborating with renowned designers and stylists, confirming my decision to pursue a career in professional styling.

Work Ethic

My work ethic is based on principles of professionalism, individualized approach, and passion for what I do. Every interaction with a client is an opportunity for me to create something unique and help them unleash their style.

I always strive for excellence, keeping up with the latest trends and continually expanding my knowledge in the field of fashion and style. My attention to detail and ability to adapt to different styles allow me to create outfits that accentuate the individuality of each client.

Professional Journey

My journey as a stylist began with obtaining education in fashion and style at the prestigious Paris Fashion University. Here, I delved into the art of styling and studied various aspects of the fashion industry.

Throughout my career, I've had the pleasure of working with diverse clients, from celebrities to everyday people, helping them find their unique style and feel confident in their skin. My achievements include participation in fashion shows, photo shoots for glossy magazines, and creating distinctive looks for clients.
